Computer Forensics and Your Information

Computer Forensics is the use of specialized techniques for recovery, authentication, and analysis of electronic data when a case involves issues relating to reconstruction of computer usage, examination of residual data, authentication of data by technical analysis, or explanation of technical features of data and computer usage. Computer Forensics requires specialized expertise that goes beyond normal data collection and preservation techniques available to end-users or system support personnel. Many people assume that information that is deleted or lost is unrecoverable. When Computer Forensics entered the world of Information Technology, deleted no longer meant deleted.
